# For invited guests (co-hosts)

*This page is public, but it's written for the experts we invite to run a meeting. Participants may read it — it's just not for them.*

Thank you for bringing your craft into the room. Here's how we work, so your meeting lands precisely.

## What this community is

**Another Way** is a live community for people who work with people. The through-line of everything we do is one move: **dissolving the false dilemma.** People arrive convinced they have two bad options; we help them find the third — the one hidden in the framing they accepted without noticing.

Your field can be anything — negotiation, money, the body, focus, leadership, conflict, craft. The lens is one: *where is the lie in how this person framed their limitation, and what does the third way look like?*

## What we ask of every guest

**Give the participant something they can apply Monday morning — not only something they can feel.**

Inspiration evaporates by Tuesday. A small, concrete practice or reframing stays. Aim to leave each person with a move they'll actually make.

## How a meeting runs

- **A short input** from you (10–20 min) — an idea, in plain language, no walls of jargon.
- **Practice** — most of the time. Pairs, small groups, or a live breakdown on the chair. Roughly half input, half practice.
- **Close with action** — every block ends with the person doing something, not just understanding.

## Non-negotiable

1. **Psychological safety first.** If the room becomes unsafe, we stop and protect it before anything else.
2. **Confidentiality.** What's said in the room stays in the room — for you too.
3. **No selling or recruiting.** Not your product, not your worldview. You're here to serve the person across from you, not to convert them. If you have an offer, discuss it with the host separately.
4. **The host holds the frame.** During the meeting the host protects the room and the timing. You bring the craft; the host holds the container.
5. **Serve, don't perform.** The best moment is when the *participant* says the insight in their own words, not when you deliver a brilliant answer. Hold the space more than you fill it.

## Tone

Simple, human, from the heart. Our people often arrive stuck in their heads. We bring them back to the concrete and the real. Speak the way the books that actually changed you are written: simply.

If this matches how you work, we'd love to invite you.
