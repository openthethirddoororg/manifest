# Join

This is a closed format of live breakdowns. We work honestly and deep, so we don't invite everyone — only those who resonate with how we work.

The form has a few questions. They aren't there to be "passed": they help *you* feel and decide — not with your head, but in agreement with yourself — whether you want to be part of this now. There are no "right" answers here: there's a match or a mismatch. If somewhere in the form you realize this isn't yours right now, that's a good result, not wasted time.

**This is probably NOT for you right now if:**

- you want to listen and watch from the side;
- you want ready answers, not your own work;
- you're not ready for direct feedback about yourself.

**This is your place if** you're ready to share your story, to see your conflict from the outside, and to rehearse the hard move without fear of judgment.
