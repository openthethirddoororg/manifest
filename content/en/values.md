# Values

This is the air the community breathes. By it, it's easy to tell whether this place is for you or not.

---

**Psychological safety** — non-negotiable. What's said in the room stays in the room. We don't judge, blame, or needle. You choose how deep you go.

**Air** — a space where everyone can show up. Without air, no one opens — that's why safety here is not up for debate.

**Honesty** — truth over comfort. First, honesty with yourself. We name what is, gently and without games. And whoever leads goes first: not perfect, gets things wrong, says so out loud.

**Vulnerability** — strength is taking the armor off. You don't have to be polished here — you have to be real. Falling and getting up isn't a glitch in the path; it is the path.

**Silence** — from a churning sea of ideas to ripples on still water. Before deciding — return to silence and listen. First we hear the person, then we look for the move. Here, silence is a tool, not a pause.

**The third door** — there's always another way out. We don't pick between two bad options — we look for the lie in the framing itself. Step out of your own point of view into someone else's and see what's invisible from inside.

**Action** — insight without a step evaporates. We close every breakdown with something the person actually does. You're responsible for the move, not the outcome: take the step — release the result. We put into practice what resonates within 72 hours, to anchor it at the level of action.

**Your own path**
We walk alongside, not ahead. Your own pace, your own depth. No one is woken by force or pulled along — there are no gurus here and no followers. Your life, your wheel.

**Dignity**
In the room there is no big and small. Owner and employee, expert and newcomer are equal — status stays at the door. We serve the person across from us; we don't perform at them.

---

If these values resonate, you'll recognize yourself. If not, that's a good question to ask yourself — what exactly doesn't fit for me? We're open to suggestions: you can choose a different community, or [propose an improvement](https://github.com/openthethirddoororg/manifest/issues).
