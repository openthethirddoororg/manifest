# Values

These aren't rules — the rules are a separate document. This is the air the community breathes. By them it's easy to tell whether this is your place or not.

---

**Air**
A space where everyone can show up. Without air, no one opens — so safety here is not up for debate. You choose how deep you go; what's said in the room stays in the room. Breathing is allowed.

**Honesty**
Truth over comfort. First, honesty with yourself. We name what is, gently and without games. And whoever leads goes first: not perfect, gets things wrong, says so out loud.

**Vulnerability**
Strength is taking the armor off. You don't have to be polished here — you have to be real. Falling and getting up isn't a glitch in the path; it is the path.

**Silence**
From a churning sea of ideas to ripples on still water. Before deciding — return to silence and listen. First we hear the person, then we look for the move. Here, silence is a tool, not a pause.

**The third door**
There's always another move. We don't pick between two bad options — we look for the lie in the framing itself. Step out of your own point of view into someone else's and see what's invisible from inside.

**Action**
Insight without a step evaporates by Tuesday. We close every breakdown with something the person actually does. You're responsible for the move, not the outcome: take the step — release the result.

**Your own path**
We walk alongside, not ahead. Your own pace, your own depth. No one is woken by force or pulled along — there are no gurus here and no followers. Your life, your wheel.

**Dignity**
In the room there is no big and small. Owner and employee, expert and newcomer are equal — status stays at the door. We serve the person across from us; we don't perform at them.

---

If these values are yours, you'll recognize yourself. If not, that's an honest sign this isn't your place for now. Either one is fine.
